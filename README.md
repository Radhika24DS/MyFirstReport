# MyFirstReport
I created One Simple Dashboard Report in Microsoft Power BI , which visualizes on Chocalate Sales over different Countries.
Chocolate Sales Analysis Dashboard – Power BI
1. Project Title / Headline

Global Chocolate Sales Analytics: Country-wise Performance Dashboard

An interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze chocolate sales performance across different countries, products, and time periods.

2. Short Description / Purpose

The Chocolate Sales Analysis Dashboard is a dynamic Power BI report developed to explore global chocolate sales data across multiple countries. It focuses on understanding sales trends, product performance, and regional distribution. The dashboard helps business stakeholders identify high-performing markets, track product demand, and analyze sales patterns over time for better decision-making.

3. Tech Stack

The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:

Power BI Desktop – Used for creating interactive dashboards and reports

Power Query – Data cleaning and transformation of raw sales data

DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) – Used for calculated measures such as Total Sales, Orders, and KPIs

Data Modeling – Structured relationships between sales attributes like country, product, and date

File Formats – .pbix for development and .png for dashboard previews

4. Data Source

Source: Chocolate Sales Dataset

The dataset contains transactional sales data with the following attributes:

Sales Person – Name of the salesperson handling the transaction

Product – Different chocolate products (e.g., Dark Bites, Fruit & Nut Bars, Choco Almonds)

Country – Sales regions (e.g., UK and other countries)

Date – Transaction date for time-based analysis

Amount – Total revenue generated per transaction

Boxes – Quantity of products sold

Order Status – Delivery status of orders

This dataset enables detailed analysis of product demand, country-wise sales performance, and time-based trends.

5. Features / Highlights
• Business Problem

Chocolate companies operate across multiple countries and product lines, but analyzing which products perform best in which regions can be challenging. Without proper visualization, it becomes difficult to track sales trends, identify top markets, and optimize sales strategies.

• Goal of the Dashboard

The main objectives of this dashboard are:

Analyze chocolate sales across different countries

Identify top-selling products

Track sales trends over time

Evaluate salesperson and order performance

Support data-driven business decisions

• Walkthrough of Key Visuals

KPI Cards:
Display key metrics such as Total Sales Amount, Total Orders, and Total Quantity (Boxes), providing a quick overview of business performance.

Sales Trend Line Chart:
Shows how sales revenue changes over time (daily/monthly), helping identify peak sales periods and trends.

Country-wise Sales Bar Chart:
Compares sales across different countries, highlighting high-performing regions.

Product Performance Chart:
Displays which chocolate products generate the most revenue and demand.

Salesperson Analysis Chart:
Provides insights into individual salesperson performance and contribution to total sales.

Order Status Visualization:
Shows the distribution of delivered vs pending orders, helping monitor operational efficiency.

• Business Impact & Insights

This dashboard enables businesses to:

Identify top-performing countries and markets

Understand customer product preferences

Track sales growth and seasonal trends

Improve sales strategies and inventory planning

Monitor salesperson performance and delivery efficiency

By transforming raw transactional data into meaningful insights, this dashboard helps organizations optimize global chocolate sales and improve overall business performance.
